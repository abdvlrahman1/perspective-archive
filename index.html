afterall, success is a perspective

we seek for wins all throughout our lives, just to realize it's not what we really wanted. even with all the recognition and accolades, we would still want something else. the life we envisioned a few years ago, might already been different to what we need now. as we age, and wage war for so long, it dawned to us, we filter what we allow.

you know what, sometimes we need is not success, but to know that, someone will be there if ever we fail— an assurance that even if the world be ending, someone will be there for us, more than willing.
